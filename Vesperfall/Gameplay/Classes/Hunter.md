Mechanical representation of the Hunter faction. See narrative identity: [[World/Factions/Hunter]]

## Core Traits
- Balanced player-character focus with strategic support tools
- Versatile combat style; able to adapt to multiple situations
- Moderate unit deployment and environmental interaction
- Tactical gameplay emphasizing positioning and resource use
- Flexible solo and cooperative potential
