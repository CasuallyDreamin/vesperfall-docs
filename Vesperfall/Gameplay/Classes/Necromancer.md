Mechanical representation of the Necromancer faction. See narrative identity: [[World/Factions/Necromancer]]

## Core Traits
- Player-character focused with integrated units
- Resource-driven magic gameplay
- Emphasizes skillshots, area control, and strategic timing
- Requires careful resource management
- Progressive power growth through transformation
