Mechanical representation of the Vampire faction. See narrative identity: [[World/Factions/Vampire]]

## Core Traits
- Selfish
- Player-focused
- Active combat-heavy (requires micro-management)
- Explosive gameplay
- Boss-fight style character

## Player Character
[[The Vampire Lord]]

### Key Focuses
1. Abilities revolve around combos, quick reflexes, and constant engagement in battle.
2. Minimal base-building; all upgrades, buildings, tech, and bonuses directly enhance the Vampire Lord.
3. The most mechanically demanding class.
4. High risk / high reward playstyle.
5. Minimal unit production; produced units primarily serve to empower the Vampire Lord.
