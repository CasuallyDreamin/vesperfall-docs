## 1. [[Entities]]
Anything that exists in the game. from mobs and characters to areas and abstract entities like time.
## 2. [[States]]
The state of entities at any given moment. 
## 3. [[Actions]]
Things to be performed by entities.
## 4. [[Resources]]
Anything numeric about entities. ex: health, XP, character stats, actual in game resources)
## 5. [[Rules]]
Rules that dictate actions, state changes, progression and else. includes also things like restrictions, reward/punishment, conditions and the like.
## 6. [[Progression]]
Character progression from ability trees to base building progression.
