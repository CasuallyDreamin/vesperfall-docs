#root #story

Theme and feel of the whole project
## Core Themes
- Survival under scarcity
- Competition vs cooperation
- Improvement / growth
- Tension and risk

## Mood / Tone
- Suspenseful
- Chaotic but strategic
- Punishing and rewarding
- Player-driven tension

## Narrative Questions
- How far will players cooperate before pursuing victory?
- Can survival coexist with ambition?
- What strategies emerge under constant competition?

## Gameplay-Driven Themes
- Resource scarcity → tension/strategy
- PvP vs PvE → moral ambiguity
- Incremental improvement → satisfying growth
- Environmental challenge → adaptation

## Aesthetic / Style Notes
- Dangerous, lush island
- Isometric tactical view
- Minimal, clear UI
- Audio emphasizes tension
